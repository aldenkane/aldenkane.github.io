EE40458: 1 GHz Low Noise Amplifier

Fall 2019

University of Notre Dame

  • Designed a Low Noise Amplifier (LNA) for a center frequency of 1 GHz using hand calculations, Smith Charts, and Keysight's Advanced Design System (ADS) software
  • Used quarter-wave matching networks on input and output for resonance at 1 GHz
  • Analyzed gain, noise, and stability of LNA using equipment in Dr. Patrick Fay's lab at Notre Dame
  • Term project for EE 40458 (RF and Microwave Circuits for Wireless Communications)
